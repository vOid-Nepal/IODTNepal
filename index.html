About Us

Information Technology for all.

IODT is a non-government organization established to embrace the knowledge and use of computer among students in rural areas. IODT Nepal is coming up with a program aiming to provide computer education to the students of community school. This program is aimed to provide practical knowledge relating with computer. All the interesting facts and knowledge about computers that students have heard or learned, we want to make sure they are able to relate and understand its practical application and further more. We all know the importance and use of computers in today’s competitive world and no one wants their students to be any less in this competition. Students are the future of our country but they are not as privileged as the students in foreign country. In today’s world, there are lots of news on technology we hear every day and it surprises us most of the times. So, for the students who are eager to explore more, understand more, we don’t want them to be deprived of the opportunity. We believe computer knowledge is a very important aspect for a student to evolve his/her knowledge. But most of the students in our country lack this opportunity. So, we have come up with a program where we can help as many students as possible.
